PrimeScale — People Prime candidate import template
==========================================

Download: people-prime-candidate-import.csv
Open in Excel or Google Sheets. Save as CSV before uploading.

REQUIRED COLUMNS
----------------
full_name          Candidate's full name
email              Unique work email (one account per email)
headline           Short professional headline
skills             Comma-separated list, e.g. AWS,Kubernetes,Terraform
role_categories    Pipe-separated, must match: AI / ML | Cloud | Data | DevOps | Cybersecurity | Full-stack | Backend | Frontend | Mobile

OPTIONAL COLUMNS
----------------
phone
current_title
years_experience   Number, e.g. 8
experience_level   junior | mid | senior | lead  (default: mid)
salary_min         Number, annual USD
salary_max         Number, annual USD
work_authorization us_citizen | green_card | h1b | ead | international_remote  (default: international_remote)
location           e.g. Remote, India, CA
preferred_work_type remote | hybrid | onsite  (default: remote)
availability       actively_looking | open | not_looking  (default: actively_looking)
bio
linkedin_url
github_url
portfolio_url

TIPS
----
- Keep the header row exactly as in the template.
- Use quotes around skills if they contain commas: "AWS,Kubernetes,Terraform"
- Use | between role categories: DevOps|Cloud
- Delete the sample rows before uploading your real data, or replace them.
